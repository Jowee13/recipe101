 Tuna Sisig

Simple recipe by Sambon

This is a tuna sisig which my dad cooks if he feels like cooking it and I am here to share the recipe which is simple

The recipe is simple and can be done within 15 mins and is yummy.

Ingredients

Serves 2-3 people

  • 2 cans Tuna

  • 3 freshpepper

  • 1 Jar Mayo

  • 2 Tbsp oil

  • 1 Tbsp sugar

Preparation

  • Prepare the ingredients
  • Open can of tuna and mayo and pour
  • mix well then turn on heat and set to low
  • pour oil and mix with the pepper
  • continue to mix until you could smell the aroma then just heat it but not to much
  • Serve
